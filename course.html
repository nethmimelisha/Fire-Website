FIRE &
RESCUE COURSE

Be a Professional in Fire and Rescue field having completion with NVQ Level 3-4 Standard Certification.

Course Time: 02 Months (200 Hours) -Weekdays & Weekend



Be a Professional Level Fire Fighter.

Join with foreign fire services or companies and work as fire fighter.

Entry Requirements
• Should be in Good Health Condition
• Minimum height 160cm and comply with BMI
• Age limit 18 - 30 years
• Medical Certificate ensuring your fitness for the course



Course Line-Up

Practical

• Physical endurance training
• Hose and pumps drills
• Smoke house drill
• Casualties handling and management
• Rope Rescue and confines spaces Rescue
• Dynamics risk assessment and safety procedures

Theory

• Principles in Fire Safety
• Fire Prevention
• Fighting fire techniques
• Fire Evacuation procedure
• Equipment and vehicles maintenance
• Incident command system
