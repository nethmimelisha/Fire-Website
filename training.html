Training Department
 

The mission is to equip today's firefighters and emergency responders with the latest techniques, skills, and technical training. Guides are given to carry out operations under a realistic and challenging environment, allowing the firemen to work together with expert advisors.

Sharing knowledge online with fire service professionals scattered around the world is another function of this. The intention of this department is to produce officers who have very good endurance in the fire service and can perform operations safely in critical situations. Also, this division provides training programs in fire, rescue, and first aid to various private and government organizations.

 

 

Role of Training Section

1. Internal Training
2. External Training

 

Internal Training

• Conducting day to day training
• Recruitment training ( One year )
• All promotion training courses
• Updating training modules
• Rescue training courses
• B.A. Training courses
• HAZMAT training courses
• First aid Training

 

External Training

• Fire fighting awareness training course – one day
• Basic fire fighting and rescue training course – 03 day
• Basic fire fighting and rescue training course – 05 day
• Rescue training course – 03day,05 day (Rope & high angle rescue / Road traffic incident rescue/ CSSR & confined space rescue)
• Fire safety management training course – one day
• First aid Training - 03 day
• BA Training
• Hazmat Training (CBRNE)
